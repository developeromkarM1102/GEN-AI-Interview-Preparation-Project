# 🚀 GEN-AI Interview Preparation Platform

## 🌟 Overview

GEN-AI Interview Preparation Platform is a smart, AI-powered web application designed to help students and job seekers prepare for interviews efficiently and confidently.

Instead of following generic preparation methods, this platform creates a **personalized interview experience** based on:

* Your **Resume**
* Your **Self Description**
* The **Job Description** you're targeting

It acts like a **virtual interview coach**, guiding you step-by-step with tailored insights, questions, and feedback.

---

## 💡 Problem It Solves

Most candidates:

* Don’t know what to prepare
* Practice random interview questions
* Lack structured feedback
* Struggle to align preparation with specific job roles

👉 This platform solves that by using AI to generate a **focused and personalized interview preparation plan**.

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

### 🤖 AI-Powered Interview Plan

* Generates a customized preparation roadmap
* Based on resume, job role, and personal background
* Focuses on relevant skills and topics

### 📄 AI Resume Builder (ATS-Friendly)

* Automatically generates a professional resume
* Optimized for **ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)**
* Clean, modern, and recruiter-friendly format

### 📊 Performance Tracking

* Tracks your interview preparation progress
* Shows performance insights
* Helps identify strengths and weak areas

### 🧠 Smart Question Generation

* Generates interview questions tailored to your profile
* Covers technical + behavioral aspects

### 🕘 Recent Reports Access

* Users can revisit previous interview reports
* No need to start from scratch every time

## 🧩 How It Works

1. User provides:

   * Resume (PDF)
   * Job Description
   * Self Description

2. AI processes the data

3. Platform generates:

   * Personalized Interview Plan
   * AI Resume
   * Interview Questions
   * Performance Insights

4. User can:

   * Practice
   * Track progress
   * Access past reports
